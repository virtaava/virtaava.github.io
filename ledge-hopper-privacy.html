Ledge Hopper — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Summary

Ledge Hopper is a casual mobile game. Your game progress stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information ourselves. The game displays ads served by Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers as described below.

Data We Collect

None directly. Ledge Hopper does not collect personal data, account information, location, contacts, photos, or analytics. The app does not require sign-in.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves the following locally to user://ledge_hopper_save.dat on your device only:

  • Best score and lifetime games played
  • Selected bunny character and owned-character entitlements (which paid bunny packs you have purchased)
  • Sound and music on/off toggles, and language preference
  • Lifetime experience points
  • Carrot wallet balance and Royal Vault collection state
  • Daily quest progress, current streak day count, last-completion date, and milestone-claim state
  • First-seen flags for unlockable biomes and other one-time tutorial toasts

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly tap the "Share My Bunny" or "Share Score" buttons, which save a screenshot to your device's Pictures folder and copy a short text message to your clipboard for you to paste into a sharing app of your choice.

Advertising

Ledge Hopper displays ads provided by Google AdMob:

  • A banner ad anchored to the bottom of most screens
  • An interstitial (full-screen) ad after every third retry
  • An optional rewarded video ad you can choose to watch to continue a run

AdMob may collect device identifiers (advertising ID), approximate location derived from IP, and usage data to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's Privacy Policy and Google's policies for partner sites. You can reset or limit your advertising ID at any time in your device's Settings → Privacy → Ads.

Consent for Personalized Ads (EEA, UK, Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the app uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) consent SDK to show a consent form on first launch (and again if Google updates its vendor list). You can choose whether to allow personalized advertising. If you decline, AdMob will serve only non-personalized ads, which use a minimal set of identifiers for frequency capping and fraud prevention.

You can review or change your choice at any time by tapping Settings → Manage Privacy inside the app. Users outside these regions do not see the consent form because it is not required by law in their region; ads are served per AdMob's regional defaults.

In-App Purchases

The app supports optional in-app purchases handled by Google Play Billing. We do not see or store your payment information; all billing is processed by Google. The catalog consists of:

  • Five themed bunny character packs — Hanbok Idol, Mooncake Lantern, Pixel Arcade, Witchling Mage, and Steampunk. Each is a one-time purchase that unlocks the corresponding painted bunny skin.
  • Three carrot packs — consumable in-game currency bundles you can use for in-game conveniences.

The only data the app stores about purchases is a local entitlement bitmask of which character packs you own and your carrot wallet balance. All payment processing is handled by Google Play. See Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google Play handles purchase data.

Children's Privacy

Ledge Hopper is intended for general audiences but is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. AdMob is configured with content rating "PG" or below for all users.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — ad serving. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) — consent management for users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland. Bundled with the AdMob SDK. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy

No other third-party services are used. The game does not send any telemetry, crash reports, or analytics to our servers because we do not operate any servers for this game.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data, including your save file and carrot wallet. There is no server-side data to delete because we don't collect any. Purchase records remain with Google Play and can be managed at play.google.com/store/account.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be published at this URL with an updated date. The app itself does not auto-update the policy.
