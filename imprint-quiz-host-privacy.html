Imprint Quiz Host — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Summary

Imprint Quiz Host is a local-first application. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information.

Data We Collect

None. The app does not collect any personal data, usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following locally on your device only:

  • Player profile (display name, avatar choice)
  • Game progress (level, streaks, quiz history)
  • Settings (theme preference, sound toggle, AI provider configuration)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share a screenshot or share card using your phone's standard sharing features.

AI Provider (Optional)

If you choose to connect an external AI provider for question generation, the app sends quiz-related prompts (category, difficulty level) to the provider URL you configure. No personal data is included in these requests. The app does not store or transmit your API key to any party other than the provider you specify.

Advertising

The free version displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can remove all advertising by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option within the app. The paid version makes zero network calls beyond the optional AI provider you configure.

Children's Privacy

Imprint Quiz Host is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (free version only) — ad serving. Google Privacy Policy
  • User-configured AI provider (optional) — question generation only, no personal data sent

No other third-party services are used.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. There is no server-side data to delete because we don't collect any.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be published at this URL with an updated date. The app itself does not auto-update the policy.
